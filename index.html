Our Company

Corfu Sup Rental resides near Nissaki's port which lies on the northen side of Corfu Island and is easily accessible by foot or vehicle. We are open for business for the whole duration of the summer season and accept bookings for every day of the week.

Sup boards

Standup paddleboarding (SUP) is a water sport born from surfing with modern roots in Hawaii. Stand up paddleboarders stand on boards that are floating on the water, and use a paddle to propel themselves through the water.

Kayaks

A kayak is a small, narrow watercraft which is typically propelled by means of a double-bladed paddle. The traditional kayak has a covered deck and one or more cockpits, each seating one paddler.As a general rule, a longer kayak is faster.

Paddling Boats

A pedalo is a human-powered watercraft propelled by the turning of a paddle wheel. The wheel is turned by people of rotating the pedals of the craft. The paddle wheel of a pedalo is a smaller version of that used by a paddle steamer.

What We Offer

Our company specializes in the renting of recreational-activity marine vessels in the island of Corfu. These include Sup Boards, One-seat and two-seat Canoes, as well as Paddling Boats. With our experienced stuff and top quality equipment we can guarantee a very fun and unforgettable experience.

SUP Boards

SUP Boards can be driven while crouched or standing at the center of the board. A standard oar is used for maneuvering and turning the board.

Kayaks

Canoes are available in a one-seat and a two-seat version. They are made to be very steady and nimble and are propelled using a double oar.

Paddling Boats

Paddling boats are ideal and easy to use for 2-person groups. They are propelled by paddling, much like a bicycle, and are also easy to maneuver.
