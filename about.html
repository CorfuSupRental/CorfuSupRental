About Us

About Our Company

At Courfu SUP rental we offer top quality services and equipment for our clients while employing all necessary measures for ensuring a safe, while also fun and exciting, experience. Our team members are involved in the area of marine-recreational services for a vast number of years and are fully qualified for every aspect of such activities.

Set in the area of Nissaki, located on the northen side of Corfu, our company operates every day of the week for the whole duration of the summer season. For Bookings or more information about our services please refer to our Contact Information.


Our rental hardware is always of the top quality and is constantly checked and renewed when required to ensure safety and ease of use for everyone.

Our company is actively offering the following rental services to customers :


  • Renting a SUP board (comes with a standard oar.)

  • Renting a kayak (One-seat and two-seat version. Comes with a double oar.)

  • Renting a paddle boat


Why Choose Us?

When it comes to Instructor experience and equipment quality and handling our team has been continuously involved in every aspect of rental services for a number of years running successfull businesses in the island of Corfu.

We all share a common goal of providing the best services for our costumers.
